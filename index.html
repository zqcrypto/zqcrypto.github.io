About

I'm an Associate Professor at the College of Computer Science and Engineering, Northwest Normal University. My research focuses on Applied Cryptography, Access Control, Privacy Protection, and Network Protocols.

Previously, I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Technology from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics in 2025, where I was advised by Prof. Youwen Zhu and Prof. Jian Wang. I was jointly trained at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Korea University. During my doctoral study, I also worked closely with Prof. Junbeom Hur from Korea University.

Openings: I'm always happy to work with motivated master’s students interested in information security, as well as undergraduate students eager to gain research experience. If you are passionate about security and privacy research, feel free to reach out!

News:

  • We are currently working on "Decoupled and Privacy-Preserving Key Generation" which has been submitted to IEEE Transactions on Information Forensics and Security (TIFS)
  • We are currently working on "Offline Verifiable Data Deletion" which is being prepared for submission to USENIX Security.
